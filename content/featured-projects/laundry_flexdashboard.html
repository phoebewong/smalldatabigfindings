Summary: I’m tired of waiting for the on-site laundry machines at my apartment buildings and therefore, I scraped one-month data of the real-time machine usage (in Nov 2017) and that gave me a reason to try out flexdashboard.

Flexdashboard

Before this project, I have primarily used Shiny to build interactive web applications or interactive visualization. Even though I learned about the existence of flexdashboard, I never had the opportunity to use it at work. The laundry data gave me a great reason to try it out!
